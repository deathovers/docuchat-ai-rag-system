# DocuChat AI User Guide

Welcome to DocuChat AI! This guide will help you get started with chatting with your documents.

## 🌟 Getting Started

DocuChat AI allows you to upload PDF documents and ask questions about them. The AI only uses the information in your documents to answer, ensuring accuracy.

### Step 1: Uploading Documents
1. Open the DocuChat interface.
2. Click on the **Upload** button or drag and drop your PDF files.
3. Wait for the "Processing Complete" notification. Your files are now indexed and ready for questions.

### Step 2: Asking Questions
Type your question in the chat box. For best results:
- **Be Specific:** Instead of "What does this say?", try "What are the payment terms in the service agreement?"
- **Contextual Follow-ups:** You can ask follow-up questions like "Can you explain the second point in more detail?"

### Step 3: Understanding Citations
Every answer provided by DocuChat AI will include citations. They look like this: `[Filename - Page X]`. 
- This tells you exactly where the AI found the information so you can verify it yourself.

---

## ⚠️ Important Limitations

1. **Session Expiry:** To protect your privacy, your documents are stored in a temporary session. If you are inactive for more than **1 hour**, the session will expire, and you will need to re-upload your documents.
2. **PDF Only:** Currently, only PDF files are supported. Scanned images inside PDFs (OCR) are not supported in this version.
3. **Grounded Answers:** If the answer to your question is not in the uploaded documents, the AI will say "Information not found." It will not make up answers.

---

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can other users see my documents?**
A: No. Each session is isolated using a unique ID. Your documents are only accessible to your specific session.

**Q: Is there a file size limit?**
A: We recommend keeping PDFs under 50MB for optimal processing speed.

**Q: Why did the AI say it couldn't find the answer?**
A: This happens if the information isn't present in the text of the PDFs you uploaded. Ensure the PDF is text-searchable and not just a collection of images.
